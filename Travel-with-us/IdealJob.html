Job Description

In this position, you will be in charge of creating and maintaining software programmes, adding new features, and making sure that the code is of a high grade. Responsibilities

- Create scalable software applications and keep them up to date.

- Write code that is clear, effective, and well-documented.

- Deliver high-quality solutions by working together with product managers and designers to comprehend needs.

- Keep abreast with new technological developments and market trends.

- Contribute to a positive and collaborative team environment.

Qualifications

- strong background in programming in languages like Java, Python, or JavaScript

- Proficiency in front-end and/or back-end development frameworks (e.g., React, Spring, Django)

- Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git).

- A strong analytical and problem-solving capacitys.

- Strong communication and collaboration abilities.
